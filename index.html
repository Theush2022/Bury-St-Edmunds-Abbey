Why Visit?

The Abbey ruins in Bury St Edmunds have are over a 1000 years old. With tours available you are sure to be amazed by the rich and interesting history of the still standing architechtual relics


14-acres of beautiful floral gadens and landscaping. A winner of the prestigious Green Flag award and one of the UK's most visited free entry tourist attractions. There is also a childrens play area with Crazy Golf and wildlife feeding areas.


With a history starting from the 11th century this fascinating example of Gothic architecture is a site to behold both internally and externally. Tours are available and exhibits of art & Cathedral artifacts can be viewed in the Edmund Gallery & Cathedral Treasures display.
